This is where I will be reviewing the Alan Wake series created by Remedy Entertainment

Remedy Entertainment is a Finnish video game company who has created some game series with incredibly dedicated fans of the games such as the Max Payne, Alan Wake and Control series of games. The face of the company is Sam Lake who is the creative director and has had his face used for the main character of Max Payne and the tritagonist of Alan Wake 2.

The Alan Wake series so far is a trilogy of games and part of the dlc for the game Control which was also made by Remedy Entertainment. The series follows the titular character Alan Wake along with Saga Anderson who appears in Alan Wake 2 as the deuteragonist and Jesse Faden who you play as in Control and the Control dlc on their journey to stop the darkness and survive.
