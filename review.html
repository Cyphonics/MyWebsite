My Review

I loved playing the Alan Wake series of games as they had so much story to experience and think about as you go through the games. The amount of detail put in these games is incredible as they needed to think about how to fit the manuscript pieces that you find in to the areas that you go through.

I loved the first game of the series as it was a unique experience that I don't think I have ever experienced before.

I didn't enjoy American Nightmare as much because it didn't feel like it fit in with the other games in the series.

I thought Alan Wake 2 was a masterpiece of a game that had a lot of love and thought put in to it.

I have not finished the dlc for both control and Alan Wake 2 however so far, I do not enjoy the control dlc as much as the main game.

These are my thoughts on whether or not to play the main games and a score that I would give them.

Game Score Reccomend?
Alan Wake 7.5/10 Yes
Alan Wake's American Nightmare 5 Only if you want to learn the lore
Alan Wake 2 9/10 Absolutely
